Syndicates.

Syndicates allow you to diversify your portfolio and co-invest in high potential startups alongside The Ark.Fund, other angel investors and venture capital funds

What is a Syndicate?

A syndicate is a private fund that invests on a single deal. In syndicates, angel investors join a lead investor to fulfill a startup’s funding needs.

As lead investor, The Ark.Fund screens the startups, chooses the most promising ones and makes an initial capital investment to get access to the deals. Afterwards, it creates a syndicate for each startup, giving angels in the network the opportunity to invest individually on a deal by deal basis through a special purpose vehicle.

The benefits of Syndicates

Syndicates allow angels to co-invest with The Ark.Fund, other experienced angel investors and succesful venture capital firms. This increases the efficiency of the funding process so startups can focus on growing their business.

By joining The Ark.Fund’s syndicates, investors can rest assured that each startup has gone through an in-depth and rigorous due dilligince process before finally being approved by our investment committee.

With the smallest investment tickets at $3,000, syndicates also offer the advantages of diversification, as investors can participate in several deals, increasing their chances of obtaining exponential returns and reducing the probabilities of experiencing considerable losses.
